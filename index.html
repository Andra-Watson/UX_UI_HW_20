Project Overview

Travel Tribe is a travel planning app that helps simplify the process of planning
a group trip through seamless collaboration and organization all on one app.

My Responsibilities

Conduct Research, Wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping,
usability study, style guide and iteration on designs

My Role

  • UX Researcher
  • UX/UI Designer
  • Branding Design

Problem Statement

Organizing a group trip can become stressful due to varying opinions, communication issues,
disorganization, and a lack of collaborative tools.

Goals

  • Allows users to collaborate seamlessly
  • Enables users to build an itinerary
  • Allows users to vote on attractions

Empathize

Understanding the User

The aim of conducting user research is to gain insight into how participants organize group vacations, which resources
they rely on for planning, and the obstacles they encounter throughout the planning journey.

Key Insights

I compiled the user insights into an affinity diagram and further categorized the insights into similar
groups. My aim was to establish an understanding of the user collectively.

  • 88% of users prefer group travel but dislike coordinating the trip details.
  • Participants also would like to use an all in one app for travel planning. Travel Tribe should provide a seamless
    way for a group to collaborate, communicate and plan a trip.

Competitive Analysis

I performed a competitive analysis on three direct competitors to identify successful
features and pain points experienced by users.

  • Users prefer a platform that consolidates all travel documents, allows exploration of hotels and
    attractions, and features a straightforward design.
  • Users prefer an app without a subscription model and with dependable customer service.

Usability Test

The purpose of this user test is to assess whether users can smoothly navigate the app, easily complete the signup process,
and initiate their trip planning seamlessly.

  • I conducted 5 user test
  • Users found the sign-up process to be straightforward.
  • The majority of users found that the tab bar was not clear.
  • Most users had difficulty locating accommodations and the itinerary page.
